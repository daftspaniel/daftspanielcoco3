Oct 2018 - A minigame in 10 lines of BASIC by Davy Mitchell

Gameplay Video : https://youtu.be/tTXXld8Uue0

You are the sheriff of an artic outpost.

Stop the advancing polar bears from attacking the town.

Press the requested 3 keys (A to Z) to Ready! Aim! Fire!

N.B Be kind to real polar bears :-)
